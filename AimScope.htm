Aim & Scope:

IJEEE is an international peer-reviewed, Computer , online journal. The aim and scope of the journal is to provide an academic medium and an important reference for the advancement and dissemination of research results that support high-level learning, teaching and research in the fields of computer science and technology. Original theoretical work and application-based studies, which contributes to a better understanding of computer science and technological challenges, are encouraged.
